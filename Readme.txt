How To Use:
===========

1. Prepare your player portraits textures (PNG or DDS).
2. Put them on folder engine\textures.
3. On tools, enter your playerpkg (KIHINRI, STNASH_, etc).
4. Hit Launch FshX button, open players.fsh on folder engine\resources.
5. Change the textures with yours, save it and close.
6. Hit Save Works button and select folder to save it.
7. Your big files will generated by the tools.

PS: Replacing textures with FshX is more stabil than fshline. Besides this is demo version, may you'll find some bugs :)
